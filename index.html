How to Play Hadooney

🎯 Objective

Conquer territories and eliminate all opponents! Control as much land as possible to earn stars.

🗺️ Controls

Attack/Move: Tap a tile you own, then tap an adjacent tile

Recruit: Spend 10 gold to add 5 troops to any owned tile

End Turn: Click when done - all AI opponents will take their turns

⚔️ Combat

Troops battle with a random factor. Terrain affects defense:

🌾 Plains: 1.0x defense | 🌲 Forest: 1.3x defense | ⛰️ Mountain: 1.5x defense

🌊 Water: Impassable

💰 Economy

Each territory generates 1 gold per turn (forests give 2 gold!)

Spend gold to recruit troops or use your era's special power (20 gold, once per game)

⭐ Star Rating

Based on territory percentage at game end:

20%+ = ⭐ | 50%+ = ⭐⭐ | 75%+ = ⭐⭐⭐ | 85%+ = ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 94%+ = ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
