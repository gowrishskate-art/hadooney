# Hadooney — Empire Wars

## What is Hadooney Empire Wars?
A hex-based empire conquest strategy game set across 20 different eras — from Ancient Egypt to the Frost Dominion. Conquer territories, use unique special powers, and outsmart AI opponents!

## How to Play
1. Choose your era (each has a unique special power)
2. Pick how many AI opponents (1-20)
3. Conquer the hex map!
   - Click your tile, then click an adjacent tile to attack
   - Spend gold to recruit troops (10g = 5 troops)
   - Use your era's special power (costs 20g, once per game)
   - Click END TURN when done

## Star Ratings
Conquer territory to earn stars:
- 1 star: 20% of the map
- 2 stars: 50%
- 3 stars: 75%
- 4 stars: 85%
- 5 stars: 94%

## The 20 Eras
**Historical:** Ancient Egypt, Roman Empire, Viking Age, Medieval Europe, Ottoman Empire, Aztec Empire, Ming Dynasty, Edo Japan, British Empire, Industrial Revolution

**Fictional:** Cyber Dynasty, Quantum Realm, Star Empire, Dragon Dominion, Crystal Kingdom, Shadow Nexus, Thunder Legion, Void Collective, Ember Ascendancy, Frost Dominion

## Features
- Hex-based tactical map with 4 terrain types
- 20 unique era powers (Sandstorm, Dragon Breath, Orbital Strike...)
- 4 AI personalities (Aggressive, Defensive, Balanced, Cunning)
- Play time tracker across sessions
- Star ratings saved per era
- Works on Android phone + MacBook
- No internet needed (fully offline)

## Tech Stack
- HTML5 Canvas (game rendering)
- JavaScript (game logic)
- CSS (UI/menus)
- GitHub Pages (hosting)
- localStorage (save data)

## Play It
Open `index.html` in any browser — no server needed!
